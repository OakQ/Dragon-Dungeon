Paddle Dungeon: Revenge of the Ghosts

  • High Concept: A turn based based grid strategy game where you have to collect all the booty while avoiding the ghosts
  • Player Experience Goals: Deep intellectual thought for the strategy and competetion for the highest score
  • Genre: Turn Based Strategy
  • Game play: You must move the Paddle around the arena with WASD to collect the chests in the area. You get to move one space per turn and then the ghosts take a turn. This continues until you have collected all the chests in the arena
  • Prototype goal: Turn based movement
  • Features: Turn system where your movement is more limited than in most games. Risk and Reward in regards to the ghosts random movements
  • Setting/Premise: Set in an arena made by the ghosts several years after the events of the BreakOut Ghost Rebellions
  • Story: It's been 1000 years since the Ghost Rebellions ended and after an era of peace and prosperity in the Paddle Land, the ghosts have returned for the revenge. One lone Paddle, the descendent of the Paddle who fought in the Ghost rebellions must now venture into the Spooky Arena to beat the ghosts at their own game
  • Target Audience: Ages 10-18
  • Play time: Less than 2 minutes a round
  • Strategies: Trying to keep as many tiles away from the ghost as possible while taking as few moves as possible
